<p><p>Styrenic Block Copolymers (SBCs) are versatile polymers used in various applications due to their unique properties. The market is primarily divided into three main types: Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS), Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene (SIS), and Styrene-Ethylene-Butylene-Styrene (SEBS). SBS offers excellent elasticity and is commonly used in adhesives and sealants. SIS provides better thermal stability and is utilized in personal care products. SEBS, known for its superior weather resistance, finds applications in automotive and medical industries. Additionally, other specialized SBCs cater to niche markets.</p></p>
