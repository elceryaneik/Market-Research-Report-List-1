<p><p>The diagnostic audiometer market encompasses three primary types: stand-alone audiometers, hybrid audiometers, and PC-based audiometers. Hybrid audiometers combine features of both stand-alone and PC-based systems, providing flexibility and enhanced functionality. PC-based audiometers leverage computer technology to perform comprehensive audiometric assessments, often featuring advanced software for data analysis and reporting, facilitating integration with electronic health records for streamlined patient management.</p></p>
