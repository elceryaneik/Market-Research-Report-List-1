<p><ul><li>Rotary-Hearth Type</li><li>Straight-Chamber Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Heating Furnace market comprises various types, including Rotary-Hearth and Straight-Chamber furnaces. Rotary-Hearth types utilize a circular platform for material movement, providing uniform heating and efficient processing for applications like steel and alloy production. In contrast, Straight-Chamber furnaces feature a linear design that allows continuous loading and unloading of materials, enhancing efficiency in operations such as annealing and heat treatment. Both types cater to diverse industries, offering specialized solutions for high-temperature processing with improved operational flexibility.</p></p>
