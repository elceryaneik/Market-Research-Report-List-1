<p><p>The protein purification resin market consists of several specialized types designed for isolating specific proteins. Protein A purification resin is primarily used for affinity chromatography of antibodies, exploiting the affinity of Protein A for the Fc region of immunoglobulins. Protein G purification resin offers similar capabilities but provides a higher binding capacity and is effective for a wider range of antibodies. Protein L purification resin binds to the light chains of immunoglobulins, making it suitable for purifying various antibody formats.</p></p>
