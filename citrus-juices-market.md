<p><p>The citrus juices market comprises three main types: powdered concentrate, frozen concentrate, and fresh juice. Powdered concentrate is made by dehydrating citrus juice, allowing for easy storage and transportation, while maintaining a longer shelf life. Frozen concentrate involves removing water from the juice, resulting in a compact form that can be easily reconstituted with water before consumption. Both methods cater to convenience and cost-effectiveness, appealing to manufacturers and consumers seeking alternatives to fresh juice for various culinary and beverage applications.</p></p>
