<p><p>Shielded Type Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs) are essential for constructing tunnels in challenging ground conditions. The market is segmented into Single Shielded and Double Shielded TBMs. Single Shielded TBMs feature a single shield that offers protection during tunneling, suitable for softer soils. In contrast, Double Shielded TBMs have both front and rear shields, enhancing stability and pressure control in challenging geological conditions, making them ideal for urban environments or areas with high groundwater levels. Both types cater to different tunneling needs.</p></p>
