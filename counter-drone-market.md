<p><p>The Counter Drone Market comprises three main types: Detection, Disruption, and Destruction. Detection involves technologies and systems that identify and track unauthorized drones, often using radar, cameras, or signal analysis. Disruption includes methods that interfere with a drone's communication or navigation systems, effectively neutralizing its operations without physical destruction. Lastly, Destruction involves physical means to eliminate the drone, such as ammunition or anti-drone projectiles. Each type plays a crucial role in protecting sensitive areas from drone threats.</p></p>
