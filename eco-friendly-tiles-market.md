<p><p>The eco-friendly tiles market encompasses various sustainable materials, each offering unique benefits. Ceramic tiles are made from natural clay and can be recycled, making them a sustainable choice. Travertine is a natural stone that requires minimal processing, reducing its environmental impact. Porcelain tiles are dense and durable, often sourced from recycled materials. Terrazzo combines chips from recycled glass or stone with resin, promoting sustainability. Lastly, glass tiles are crafted from recycled glass, offering vibrant colors and reducing landfill waste.</p></p>
