<p><ul><li>Primary Synthetic Graphite</li><li>Secondary Synthetic Graphite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic graphite powder market is divided into two main types: primary and secondary synthetic graphite. Primary synthetic graphite is produced directly through the high-temperature graphitization of carbon-rich materials, offering high purity and superior electrical conductivity, making it ideal for applications in batteries and electrodes. Secondary synthetic graphite, on the other hand, is derived from the reprocessing of used synthetic graphite materials. This type focuses on recycling, often being more cost-effective while providing good performance for various industrial applications.</p></p>
