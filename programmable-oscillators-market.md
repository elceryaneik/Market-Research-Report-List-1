<p><strong>The Programmable Oscillators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole Programmable Oscillators</li><li>Surface Mount Programmable Oscillators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The programmable oscillators market is segmented into two main types: through-hole and surface mount programmable oscillators. Through-hole programmable oscillators are designed for insertion into pre-drilled holes on a circuit board, offering robust connections and stability, making them ideal for applications requiring durability. Conversely, surface mount programmable oscillators are designed for mounting directly onto the surface of PCBs, allowing for smaller and lighter designs, which are increasingly preferred in compact electronic devices. Both types serve varied applications in consumer electronics, telecommunications, and automotive sectors.</p></p>
