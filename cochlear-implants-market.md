<p><ul><li>Unilateral</li><li>Binaural</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cochlear implants market can be categorized into unilateral and binaural types. Unilateral cochlear implants are designed for individuals with hearing loss in one ear, providing auditory input to that side, while preserving some natural hearing in the opposite ear. Binaural cochlear implants involve the use of devices in both ears, enhancing sound localization and overall auditory perception. The demand for each type varies based on patient needs, preferences, and the degree of hearing impairment, influencing market trends and growth.</p></p>
