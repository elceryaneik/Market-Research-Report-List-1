<p><p>The proppants market consists of three primary types: frac sand proppants, resin-coated proppants, and ceramic proppants. Frac sand proppants are natural grains used to enhance oil and gas extraction during hydraulic fracturing. Resin-coated proppants feature a coating that improves performance and reduces fines, making them more durable. Ceramic proppants, made from synthetic materials, offer superior strength and conductivity, ideal for high-pressure environments. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing resource recovery in petroleum extraction processes.</p></p>
