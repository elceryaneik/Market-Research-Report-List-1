<p><p>Data visualization tools are categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based tools are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, easy collaboration, and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premise tools are installed locally on a company's hardware, providing greater control over data security and compliance but often requiring higher initial investment and maintenance. Both types serve the purpose of transforming complex data into visual insights for better decision-making.</p></p>
