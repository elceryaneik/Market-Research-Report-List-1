<p><p>The thermal cracked gasoline market includes two main types: Raw Pyrolysis Gasoline (RPG) and Hydrogenated Pyrolysis Gasoline (HPG). RPG is a byproduct of thermal cracking processes, primarily used in petrochemical production and as a feedstock for further refining. In contrast, HPG is produced by hydrogenating RPG, which enhances its stability and quality, making it suitable as a cleaner fuel alternative. Both types cater to varying industrial needs, emphasizing their roles in energy and chemical industries.</p></p>
