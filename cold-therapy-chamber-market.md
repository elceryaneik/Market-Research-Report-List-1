<p><p>The cold therapy chamber market is divided into two main types: localized and whole-body therapies. Localized cold therapy targets specific areas of the body to reduce pain and inflammation, often using devices like ice packs or cryo chambers designed for single limbs or joints. In contrast, whole-body cold therapy involves immersion in a cryogenic chamber, exposing the entire body to extremely low temperatures for rejuvenation, recovery, and overall wellness. Both types are gaining popularity in health and wellness sectors.</p></p>
