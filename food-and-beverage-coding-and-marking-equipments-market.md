<p><p>The Food and Beverage Coding and Marking Equipment market includes various technologies for product identification and tracking. Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) Technology utilizes ink droplets for high-speed printing. Laser Technology uses focused light for permanent markings. Thermal Transfer Overprinting (TTO) Technology employs heat to transfer ink from ribbons. Print and Apply Labeling Method (PALM) applies printed labels automatically. Piezoelectric Inkjet (PIJ) and Thermal Inkjet (TIJ) Technologies use inkjet systems for precise marking, while Valve Inkjet (VIJ) offers high-volume applications. Primary Packaging Codes focus on coding directly on packaging for traceability and compliance.</p></p>
