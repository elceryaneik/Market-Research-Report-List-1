<p><p>The mechanical distance meter market includes two primary types: scale type distance meters and rotary distance meters. Scale type distance meters utilize graduated scales for measuring distances based on physical tape or marked rods, ideal for short-range applications. In contrast, rotary distance meters employ a rotating wheel or drum to measure longer distances, providing greater versatility and accuracy. Both types cater to various industries such as construction, surveying, and agriculture, enhancing efficiency in measuring land and material distances.</p></p>
