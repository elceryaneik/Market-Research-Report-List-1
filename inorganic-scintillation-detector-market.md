<p><strong>The Inorganic Scintillation Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Scintillation Detector</li><li>Liquid Scintillation Detector</li><li>Gaseous Scintillation Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inorganic scintillation detector market consists of three primary types: solid, liquid, and gaseous scintillation detectors. Solid scintillation detectors utilize crystalline materials to convert high-energy radiation into light, offering high sensitivity and resolution. Liquid scintillation detectors use liquid organic solvents that emit light when ionizing radiation interacts, making them effective for detecting low-energy beta particles. Gaseous scintillation detectors operate in gas phase, detecting radiation through scintillation from excited gas atoms, typically offering fast response times and ease of calibration.</p></p>
