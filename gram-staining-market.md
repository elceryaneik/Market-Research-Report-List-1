<p><p>Gram staining is a fundamental laboratory technique used to classify bacteria into two main groups: Gram-positive and Gram-negative. This differentiation is based on the bacteria's cell wall composition, which affects their response to the Gram stain reagents. The process involves application of a crystal violet dye, followed by iodine treatment, alcohol decolorization, and counterstaining with safranin. This method is crucial for microbiological analysis, infection diagnosis, and guiding antibiotic treatment decisions.</p><p>The Gram Staining Market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
