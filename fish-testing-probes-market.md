<p><ul><li>Single Color</li><li>Dual Color</li><li>Multi-color</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) testing probes come in three main types: single color, dual color, and multi-color. Single color probes target one specific DNA region, making them suitable for straightforward applications. Dual color probes enable simultaneous detection of two different genetic targets, enhancing diagnostic accuracy. Multi-color probes, capable of targeting multiple DNA regions at once, provide comprehensive analysis for complex genetic scenarios, aiding in the study of various cancers and genetic disorders by revealing intricate chromosomal relationships.</p></p>
