<p><p>The High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC) cables market comprises several types, primarily including mass impregnated cables and extruded cables. Mass impregnated cables utilize a paper insulation system saturated with a special compound, offering high reliability and long lifespan, ideal for underground applications. Extruded cables, on the other hand, employ polymers for insulation, providing flexibility and ease of installation. Other types may include hybrid or specialized cables designed for specific applications, catering to diverse transmission needs in the energy sector.</p></p>
