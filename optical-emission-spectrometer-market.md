<p><p>The Optical Emission Spectrometer market comprises three main types: Prism Spectrometers, Diffraction Grating Spectrometers, and Interference Spectrometers. Prism Spectrometers utilize a prism to disperse light into its constituent wavelengths, offering broad spectral analysis. Diffraction Grating Spectrometers use a grating to separate light based on wavelength, providing high resolution and sensitivity. Interference Spectrometers leverage the principle of constructive and destructive interference to measure light intensity at various wavelengths, allowing precise identification of elemental composition and concentration in samples.</p></p>
