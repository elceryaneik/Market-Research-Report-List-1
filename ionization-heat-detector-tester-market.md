<p><p>The ionization heat detector tester market features several key types, including photoelectric, ionization, dual sensor, and others. Photoelectric detectors use light reflection to sense smoke, making them effective for smoldering fires. Ionization detectors utilize radioactive material to detect fast-flaming fires. Dual sensor types combine both technologies for enhanced detection capabilities. Other market types may include specialized detectors for specific environments or applications. Each type serves to enhance safety and response in fire detection systems across various settings.</p></p>
