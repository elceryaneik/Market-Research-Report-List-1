<p><p>Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) hosting encompasses two primary market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises IaaS involves organizations deploying and managing their own hardware and software resources within their facilities, offering full control and customization. In contrast, cloud-based IaaS provides virtualized computing resources over the internet, allowing for scalability, flexibility, and reduced capital expenditures. This model enables businesses to easily adjust resources based on demand while relying on third-party providers for maintenance and support.</p></p>
