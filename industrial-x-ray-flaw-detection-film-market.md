<p><p>The Industrial X-ray Flaw Detection Film market comprises two main types: Non-Screen Type Films and Screen Type Films. Non-Screen Type Films are directly exposed to X-rays, offering high resolution and sensitivity for detecting small flaws in various materials. In contrast, Screen Type Films use a fluorescent screen to enhance X-ray exposure, allowing for faster imaging and reducing radiation dosage. Both types are essential for quality control in industries like aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, ensuring structural integrity and safety.</p></p>
