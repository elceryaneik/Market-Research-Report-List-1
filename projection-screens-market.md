<p><p>The projection screens market is primarily divided into fixed type and mobile type screens. Fixed type projection screens are mounted permanently on walls, providing a smooth, tensioned surface ideal for high-quality, long-term installations, such as in home theaters or conference rooms. In contrast, mobile type projection screens are portable and can be easily set up or taken down, making them suitable for temporary presentations, outdoor events, or mobile setups in classrooms and offices. Both types cater to diverse user needs.</p></p>
