<p><p>The Mat-Style Conveyor market features two main types: Rubber Belt and Thermoplastic Belt conveyors. Rubber Belt conveyors are known for their durability and flexibility, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications and varying environmental conditions. In contrast, Thermoplastic Belt conveyors offer lightweight, corrosion-resistant solutions that are ideal for industries requiring hygiene and easy maintenance, such as food processing. Both types provide efficient material handling but cater to different operational needs based on their material properties and applications.</p></p>
