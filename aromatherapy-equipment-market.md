<p><p>The aromatherapy equipment market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Ultrasonic Diffusers** use vibrations to create a fine mist of essential oils and water, dispersing aromatic molecules into the air. </p><p>2. **Nebulizing Diffusers** atomize essential oils directly without water, providing a potent aroma. </p><p>3. **Evaporative Diffusers** use a fan to blow air through essential oils, facilitating evaporation and scent release. </p><p>4. **Heat Diffusers** use gentle heat to warm oils, enhancing fragrance without combustion. Each type offers unique benefits for enhancing atmosphere and promoting wellness.</p></p>
