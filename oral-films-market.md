<p><strong>The Oral Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Orodispersible Film</li><li>Oromucosal Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral films market comprises two primary types: oro-dispersible films and oromucosal films. Oro-dispersible films dissolve quickly in saliva, delivering medication directly into the bloodstream through the oral cavity, making them ideal for patients with swallowing difficulties. Oromucosal films, on the other hand, are designed for localized drug delivery, adhering to the mucous membranes in the mouth and providing a sustained release of medication. Both types cater to diverse therapeutic areas, enhancing patient compliance and convenience.</p></p>
