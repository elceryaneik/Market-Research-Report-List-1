<p><p>The Composite Architectural Membrane Market is categorized into three types: transparent, semi-transparent, and opaque membranes. Transparent membranes allow maximum light transmission, ideal for structures that emphasize natural lighting. Semi-transparent membranes provide a balance, permitting some light while offering privacy, often used in modern buildings for aesthetic appeal. Opaque membranes, on the other hand, block light entirely, serving purposes such as insulation or shading. Each type caters to specific design needs, influencing energy efficiency, aesthetic appeal, and functional performance in architecture.</p></p>
